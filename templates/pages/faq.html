Frequently Asked Questions


How does the recruitment process work at PRIMELOCUM HEALTHCARE?

Our recruitment process involves an initial consultation to understand your staffing needs, followed by sourcing and vetting qualified candidates, and coordinating interviews to ensure the best fit for your organization.

What types of healthcare professionals do you provide?

We specialize in providing temporary, locum, and agency staffing for Operating Theatres and Post-Anaesthesia Care Units (PACU), including Anaesthetic Technicians and Recovery Room Practitioners.

How can candidates apply for positions through your agency?

Candidates can apply by creating an account on our website, browsing available job listings, and submitting applications directly through our platform.

What are the benefits of partnering with PRIMELOCUM HEALTHCARE?

We offer flexible staffing solutions, access to a pool of qualified professionals, and comprehensive support throughout the hiring process to ensure seamless integration into your team.

How does PRIMELOCUM HEALTHCARE ensure the quality of its candidates?

We have a rigorous screening and vetting process to ensure the highest quality standards. This includes verifying qualifications, conducting thorough background checks, obtaining professional references, and assessing relevant experience. Additionally, we ensure all candidates are compliant with healthcare regulations and possess the necessary certifications for their roles.
